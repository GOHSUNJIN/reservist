Help & FAQ
FOR RESERVISTS
How do I check in?
Open the Check-In tab and tap "Locate me" to verify your GPS location. Once verified, tap Phase 1 to record your arrival. Complete Phase 2-4 as the day progresses.
What do the four phases mean?
Phase 1: Arrive and check in to work.
Phase 2: Leave for lunch or dinner break.
Phase 3: Return from break.
Phase 4: Check out at end of shift.
How do I request an absence?
Tap the "Request absence" button on the Check-In tab. Your supervisor will approve or decline your request and you will be notified.
Can I check in if I'm offline?
Yes. Your check-in is queued and will sync automatically once your internet connection is restored.
What if the GPS fails?
Step outside to an open area with a clear view of the sky and try again. If GPS keeps failing on-site, contact your supervisor to manually record your check-in.
FOR ADMINS
How do I approve a new signup?
Go to the People tab and open the Requests sub-tab. Pending signups appear there. You can approve individually or select multiple and use "Approve selected".
How do I add someone manually?
Go to People tab, tap Roster, then tap the + button in the Personnel section. For multiple people, use the Bulk button instead.
How do I export attendance?
Go to the Log tab and tap Export CSV. A spreadsheet will download with the full cycle attendance record including per-person totals.
How do I post a notice to reservists?
Go to the Dashboard tab and tap Post notice to reservists. The message will appear as a banner on every reservist's check-in screen. Note: requires the notice_text column to be added to the batches table in Supabase.
How do I reset a reservist's password?
Go to People tab, Roster sub-tab, find the person and tap Reset PW. Enter a temporary password and send it to them securely. They can update it in their account settings.
